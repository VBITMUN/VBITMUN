About VBIT MUN

The situation that the world is currently in creates a dire need for leaders, diplomats and thinkers who are the epitome of wisdom and intellect.VBIT aims to inspire the youth to follow tenets of wisdom in all aspects of their lives through it’s first ever MUN, VBIT MUN 1.0. It holds the power and potential to unite the young minds.

Model United Nations is an educational simulation in which the students act as delegates representing various countries in an attempt to resolve complex problems by diplomacy and critical thinking.The entire model aims to provide delegates with a simulated political experience and allows for the refinement of skills in teamwork, debating, research and analysis.VBIT MUN 1.0 comprises of various committees, delicately crafted to incite critical thinking, break ideological barriers and inculcate accountability.An enthralling experience, MUNs empower students with a unique perspective and a perspicuous understanding of the idiosyncrasies of impending world affairs.

Our Vision

Prepare youth for the future and empower them to be a part of decision making at all levels.

The Mission

Our mission is to promote ‘learning by doing’ and provide a platform to build various abilities beyond academics which transform a person into a global leader.We believe that people’s involvement at all levels of decision making (local, national and global) is essential in order to achieve the goals of the United Nations.

The Goal

To build and mentor global citizens and peace negotiators for the generations using Model United Nations programs where the voice of youth is heard worldwide.

About VBIT

Vignana Bharathi Institute of Technology, is the place where opportunities are provided for students to realize their ambitions, where new ideas are born, where innovative projects materialize, and where students excel in learning.

Founded in 2004, by highly qualified people, VBIT emerged as a hub for engineering excellence. At VBIT, students discover engineering in a different light. Students experience an engineering education that is on par with the industry's requirement. The sprawling 17.78 acre campus has state-of-the-art teaching facilities to train students in emerging technologies. The college is located about 18 kms from Uppal on the Warangal Highway.
